# Fitlife Business Questions

This document contains a comprehensive set of business questions relevant for the Fitlife case, organized by **Financials** and **Customer Behavior**.

---

## 💰 FINANCIALS

### Revenue Analysis
1. **What is the total revenue for the current period?** (By month, quarter, year)
2. **What is the revenue per active member?** (Average price paid per member)
3. **How does revenue vary by membership plan?** (Basic, Premium, Family)
4. **What is the revenue breakdown by center?** (Which centers generate the most revenue?)
5. **How does revenue vary by acquisition channel?** (Which channels bring the most valuable members?)
6. **What is the revenue trend over time?** (Month-over-month, quarter-over-quarter, year-over-year)
7. **What is the impact of discounts on revenue?** (Total discount applied vs. revenue generated)
8. **What is the revenue per member by tenure?** (Do longer-tenured members pay more/less?)

### Costs Analysis
9. **What are the total variable costs (cost to serve)?** (By period, center, plan)
10. **What are the total fixed costs?** (Monthly fixed costs allocation)
11. **What is the cost per member?** (Variable and fixed costs per active member)
12. **How do costs vary by membership plan?** (Which plans are more expensive to serve?)
13. **What is the cost breakdown by center?** (Which centers have higher operational costs?)
14. **What is the cost trend over time?** (Are costs increasing or decreasing?)
15. **What is the cost per member by acquisition channel?** (Which channels have higher service costs?)

### Margins & Profitability
16. **What is the contribution margin?** (Revenue - Variable Costs)
17. **What is the contribution margin percentage?** (% of revenue)
18. **What is the net result (profit/loss)?** (Contribution Margin - Fixed Costs)
19. **What is the net margin percentage?** (% of revenue)
20. **How do margins vary by membership plan?** (Which plans are most profitable?)
21. **How do margins vary by center?** (Which centers are most profitable?)
22. **What is the margin trend over time?** (Are margins improving or deteriorating?)
23. **What is the break-even point?** (How many members needed to cover fixed costs?)
24. **What is the margin per member by acquisition channel?** (Which channels are most profitable?)

### Pricing Analysis
25. **What is the average price paid per plan?** (Basic, Premium, Family)
26. **What is the average discount applied per plan?** (Are discounts affecting profitability?)
27. **How does pricing compare to competitor prices?** (vs. competitor_lowcost_price)
28. **What is the price elasticity?** (How do price changes affect member retention?)
29. **What is the optimal pricing strategy?** (Based on retention and profitability)
30. **How do discounts impact member retention?** (Do discounted members stay longer?)

### LTV & CAC (Lifetime Value & Customer Acquisition Cost)
31. **What is the average Lifetime Value (LTV) per member?** (Total revenue - total costs per member)
32. **What is the LTV by acquisition channel?** (Which channels bring highest LTV members?)
33. **What is the LTV by membership plan?** (Which plans generate highest LTV?)
34. **What is the Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)?** (Average acquisition cost per period)
35. **What is the CAC by acquisition channel?** (Which channels have lower/higher CAC?)
36. **What is the LTV/CAC ratio?** (Is the business model sustainable?)
37. **What is the LTV/CAC ratio by channel?** (Which channels have best ROI?)
38. **How long does it take to recover CAC?** (CAC payback period by cohort)
39. **What is the CAC payback by multiple?** (When do cohorts reach 1x, 3x, 5x, 10x, 20x CAC?)
40. **How does CAC vary over time?** (Is acquisition getting more/less expensive?)
41. **What is the LTV trend by cohort?** (Are newer cohorts more/less valuable?)

### Unit Economics
42. **What is the revenue per member?** (Average monthly revenue per active member)
43. **What is the cost per member?** (Average monthly cost per active member)
44. **What is the profit per member?** (Average monthly profit per active member)
45. **What is the unit economics by plan?** (Revenue, cost, profit per member by plan)
46. **What is the unit economics by center?** (Revenue, cost, profit per member by center)
47. **What is the unit economics trend over time?** (Are unit economics improving?)

### Financial Context & External Factors
48. **How do competitor price changes affect our revenue?** (Correlation with competitor_lowcost_price)
49. **What is the impact of campaigns on revenue?** (january_promo, summer_body, back_to_gym)
50. **How do service incidents affect financial performance?** (app_outage, equipment_breakdown, heating_failure)
51. **What is the relationship between occupancy rate and revenue?** (Does higher occupancy = higher revenue?)
52. **How do fixed costs vary over time?** (Are fixed costs increasing?)

## 🎯 FILTERS & DIMENSIONS

All questions can be analyzed with the following filters:
- **Time Period**: Month, Quarter, Year, Custom range
- **Center**: All centers or specific center
- **Plan**: All plans or specific plan (Basic, Premium, Family)
- **Comparison**: Month-over-Month (MoM), Quarter-over-Quarter (QoQ), Year-over-Year (YoY)

---

## 📝 NOTES

- Questions should be analyzed considering **temporal evolution** and **seasonality**
- KPIs should be **contextualized** with:
  - Comparison vs. previous periods
  - Ratios vs. unit of analysis (e.g., per active member, per visit)
- Questions marked with **context** should consider external factors (campaigns, incidents, competitor prices)
